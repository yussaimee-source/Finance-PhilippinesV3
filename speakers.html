Keynote

Ana Maria Aboitiz Delgado

President & CEO, UnionBank of the Philippines
President, Bankers Association of the Philippines 2026–2027

Ana Maria Aboitiz-Delgado is the President and Chief Executive Officer of Union Bank of the Philippines, a role she assumed in January 2025 following more than two decades at the institution.

In March 2026 she was elected President of the Bankers Association of the Philippines (BAP) for the 2026–2027 term — the first woman to lead the organisation in its nearly 80-year history.

Aboitiz-Delgado holds a bachelor's degree from Boston College and an MBA from New York University's Stern School of Business. In 2025 she was named one of Fortune magazine's 100 Most Powerful Women in Asia.

At Finance Philippines 2026 she delivers the keynote on the future of AI in Philippine banking.

Marlon P. Sorongon

Chief Information Security Officer (CISO), Maybank Philippines

Marlon P. Sorongon is the Chief Information Security Officer of Maybank Philippines, responsible for the institution's information security strategy and cyber risk management across its Philippine operations.

He oversees defences against AI-driven fraud, industrialised scam networks, and advanced persistent threats targeting financial institutions in the Philippines and ASEAN.

At Finance Philippines 2026 he shares insights on building resilient, trust-preserving security architectures as autonomous AI systems become embedded across banking operations.
